About

Henry Lux

Henry Lux is a photographer based in New York. He is currently pursuing a bachelor’s degree in Studio Art at Hunter College, CUNY.

His background includes coursework in photography, studio lighting, and studio art at the School of Visual Arts, LaGuardia Community College, and Hunter College. He received The Arnold & Augusta Newman Foundation Scholarship at LaGuardia Community College.
